Functional Swift Conference 2018

Dec 1, NYC

On December 1, 2018, a small conference on the topic of Functional Programming in Swift happened in New York (similar to the previous). It was a day filled with awesome talks and lots of great conversation.

Speakers

Loïc Lecrenier - Fuzz Testing Swift Packages
Peter Tomaselli - Write to go faster
Jason Zurita - Compositional UI Styling
Ben Cohen - Fast Safe Mutable State
Danny Hertz - From Sketch to Xcode: Building Functional Reactive View Models from the Ground Up
Brandon Kase - Scanning for Scans
Stephen Celis - Reactive View Models, Simplified
Brandon Williams - Seemingly Impossible Swift Programs

Schedule

Join us at 9am for talks and conversations with other functional programming enthusiasts. Talks will begin at 10am, will be 30min, and the entire conference should be over around 6pm.

Venue

Betterment has kindly offered to host us, and provide snacks, lunch, and drinks. Their offices are located at 61 W 23rd St, New York, NY, close many subway and bus stations!
